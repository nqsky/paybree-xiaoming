常见问题 FAQ

如何开始交易？ How to start trading?

+
注册账户并完成身份验证后，即可在交易大厅选择合适的报价进行交易。我们提供详细的新手指南帮助您快速入门。 Register and complete KYC, then choose an offer in the trading hall. Our beginner's guide will help you get started.

交易安全如何保障？ How is my trading secured?

+
平台采用多重安全机制，包括担保交易、多重身份验证、资金托管等措施，确保每笔交易的安全。 We use escrow, multi-factor authentication, and fund custody to ensure every trade is secure.

支持哪些支付方式？ What payment methods are supported?

+
目前支持银行转账、支付宝、微信支付、PayPal 等多种支付方式，具体可用方式将根据交易对手显示。 We support bank transfer, Alipay, WeChat Pay, PayPal, and more. Available methods depend on your counterparty.

如何成为认证商家？ How to become a verified merchant?

+
需要完成高级 KYC 认证，并满足平台商家准入条件，包括保证金要求和交易记录要求等。 Complete advanced KYC and meet our merchant requirements, including deposit and trading history.
