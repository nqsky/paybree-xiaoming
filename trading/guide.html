交易指南 Trading Guide

了解如何在 Paybree 上进行交易 Learn how to trade on Paybree

第一步：选择交易对 Step 1: Select Trading Pair

在交易大厅选择您想要交易的币种和金额 Select the currency and amount you want to trade in the trading hall

第二步：确认交易 Step 2: Confirm Trade

确认交易金额和费率，点击"开始交易" Confirm the trading amount and fees, click "Start Trading"

第三步：完成支付 Step 3: Complete Payment

按照提示完成支付，等待商家确认 Complete the payment as prompted and wait for merchant confirmation

交易提示 Trading Tips

  • 请确保您的账户有足够的余额 Make sure your account has sufficient balance
  • 交易前请仔细阅读费率说明 Please read the fee schedule carefully before trading
  • 如有问题，请及时联系客服 Contact customer service if you have any questions
