隐私政策 Privacy Policy

1. 引言 1. Introduction

Paybree（以下简称"我们"）非常重视用户的隐私保护。本隐私政策旨在帮助您了解我们如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的个人信息。请您在使用我们的服务前仔细阅读本政策。 Paybree (hereinafter referred to as "we") takes user privacy protection very seriously. This Privacy Policy aims to help you understand how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. Please read this policy carefully before using our services.

2. 信息收集 2. Information Collection

2.1 主动提供的信息 2.1 Information You Provide

在您注册账户、进行身份认证或使用我们的服务时，我们会收集以下信息： When you register an account, complete identity verification, or use our services, we collect the following information:

  • 基本信息：姓名、电子邮箱、手机号码 Basic information: name, email, phone number
  • 身份认证信息：身份证件信息、面部识别信息 Identity verification information: ID document information, facial recognition information
  • 交易相关信息：银行账户信息、交易记录 Transaction-related information: bank account information, transaction records
  • 其他您主动提供的信息 Other information you voluntarily provide

2.2 自动收集的信息 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

当您使用我们的服务时，我们会自动收集： When you use our services, we automatically collect:

  • 设备信息：设备型号、操作系统、浏览器类型 Device information: device model, operating system, browser type
  • 日志信息：IP地址、访问时间、页面浏览记录 Log information: IP address, access time, page browsing history
  • 位置信息：基于IP地址的大致位置信息 Location information: approximate location based on IP address

3. 信息使用 3. Information Usage

我们收集的信息将用于： The information we collect will be used for:

  • 提供、维护和改进我们的服务 Providing, maintaining, and improving our services
  • 处理您的交易请求 Processing your transaction requests
  • 进行身份验证和风险控制 Conducting identity verification and risk control
  • 向您发送服务通知和更新 Sending service notifications and updates to you
  • 提供客户支持 Providing customer support
  • 遵守法律法规的要求 Complying with legal and regulatory requirements

4. 信息共享 4. Information Sharing

我们承诺对您的个人信息进行严格保密。除以下情况外，我们不会与第三方共享您的个人信息： We are committed to strict confidentiality of your personal information. We will not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • 获得您的明确同意 With your explicit consent
  • 为完成交易必需的信息共享 Information sharing necessary to complete transactions
  • 应法律法规或监管要求 Required by laws, regulations, or regulatory requirements
  • 为保护我们或其他用户的合法权益 To protect our or other users' legitimate rights and interests

5. 信息安全 5. Information Security

我们采取多种安全措施保护您的个人信息： We take various security measures to protect your personal information:

  • 数据加密传输和存储 Data encryption for transmission and storage
  • 访问控制和权限管理 Access control and permission management
  • 定期安全审计 Regular security audits
  • 员工保密培训 Employee confidentiality training

6. Cookie 使用 6. Cookie Usage

我们使用 Cookie 和类似技术来提供更好的服务体验。您可以通过浏览器设置管理 Cookie，但这可能会影响某些功能的使用。 We use cookies and similar technologies to provide a better service experience. You can manage cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect the use of certain features.

7. 您的权利 7. Your Rights

根据适用的法律法规，您享有以下权利： Under applicable laws and regulations, you have the following rights:

  • 访问您的个人信息 Access your personal information
  • 更正不准确的信息 Correct inaccurate information
  • 删除您的个人信息 Delete your personal information
  • 撤回同意 Withdraw consent
  • 投诉和举报 Complaints and reports

8. 未成年人保护 8. Protection of Minors

我们的服务不面向未满18周岁的未成年人。如果我们发现收集了未成年人的个人信息，我们会立即删除相关信息。 Our services are not intended for minors under 18 years of age. If we discover that we have collected personal information from minors, we will immediately delete the relevant information.

9. 政策更新 9. Policy Updates

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新后的政策将在网站上公布，并在必要时通过适当方式通知您。 We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be published on the website and you will be notified through appropriate means when necessary.

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问或建议，请通过以下方式联系我们： If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the following channels:
